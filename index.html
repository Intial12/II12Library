OF A REVAMPED HEROES

Chou

Chou, a skilled fighter with chivalry, was born in the slums and trained in ancient martial arts by a mysterious monk, excelling with nunchaku.

Read More

Rafaela

Rafaela, admired angel, and her brother Argus faced sibling rifts due to his male status. Argus fell into the Abyss, leaving Rafaela regretful but helpless.

Read More

Estes

Estes, the Elf King who has injured in the Second Plane War, fell into a deep sleep. He awoke during a storm when the Moon Elves faced the Abyss threat.

Read More

Freya (NEXT)

Freya became the Valkyrie and protector of the Northern Vale after an accident caused her predecessor's disappearance into the Twilight Orb.

Read More

Granger (RECENT)

Granger, a wandering bard and demon hunter, orphaned in the Empire-Abyss war, he harbors a deep hatred for demons. His haunting music signals impending death to them.

Read More

Phoveous

Phoveus, bewitched by Astaros, God of Terror, sacrificed his eyes for power. Now, he and Astaros venture deeper into the Dread Caves to awaken the god's body.

Read More

Yin

Yin, an orphan possessed by the Evil God Lieh, regained control but risked the god's return. To protect his loved ones, he journeyed to Moniyan Empire for a cure.

Read More

Ling

Ling, a slender swordsman, once a disciple of the Great Dragon, left in jealousy after Zilong became favored. He founded the Cyan Finch organization, seeking a final battle with Zilong.

Read More

Yu Zhong

Yu Zhong, a reincarnated Black Dragon with no memory, was awakened by Luo Yi after meeting her in the Land of Dawn. He then sought the Reverse Scale to fully reclaim his Black Dragon form.

Read More

Sun

Sun, the unruly King of the Monkeys, once imprisoned by the gods under a mountain, broke free and now seeks to showcase his skills on the continent.

Read More

Aurora

Aurora, the Maiden of the Glacier, revered in the North, endured the torment of her Frost Magic until the Light disciples granted her immortality at Queen's Peak.

Read More

Helcurt

Helcurt, an evil demon from a mysterious Abyssal race, ambushes foes from the shadows, using his poisonous staggers to deliver fatal blows.

Read More

Moskov

Moskov, a tribal prince turned Abyss warrior, grew envious of Khaleed's popularity. After nearly dying in battle and losing his family, he gave his soul to the Abyss for revenge.

Read More

X.Borg

X.Borg, once from a small village near Eruditio, admired Science and Technology. He eagerly assisted in building the High Tower, hoping it would bring change.

Read More

Johnson

Johnson, a legendary racer in Varis, broke the elite monopoly on auto racing. After winning his first official race, he mysteriously vanished, shocking the world.

Read More

Lolita

Lolita, an elven girl from a remote village, lost her parents in war and was adopted by Dr. Rooney. With a Noumenon Energy Core implanted, she gains great strength and can wield a giant mechanical hammer.

Read More

Bruno

Bruno, born into poverty and losing his legs in an accident, received artificial legs from Eruditio. In gratitude, he vowed to guard the city for life, using a football as his symbol.

Read More

Layla

After Layla receiving the Malefic Cannon from her father, her adventurous was captured by Laboratory 1718. Determined to save him, she embarked on a rescue mission with the Malefic Gun.

Read More

Nana

To control the magic within her, Nana searched for a magical fruit in the Shadow Swamp with her friend Molina. After Molina died protecting her, Nana gained the fruit and set out on a new journey with her friend's strength

Read More

Saber

Originally named Duanmeng and obsessed with kendo, Saber sought transformation at Laboratory 1718 for invincibility. After losing his memory and becoming a killing machine, he destroyed the lab and began a journey to reclaim his past.

Read More

Miya

Miya, a Moon Elf Priestess in Azrya, lived peacefully until the Demon army attacked. To protect her homeland, she led the Moon Elves in defense against the invaders.

Read More
